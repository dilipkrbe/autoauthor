# Available Reviewer Styles

The reviewer role is separate from the writer. After a draft/edit, the agent should switch to reviewer mode using the chosen reviewer to critique honestly before deciding keep/revert.

**Harsh Editor (Stephen King-inspired)**:
- Focus on pacing, tension, and emotional truth. Cut anything that slows dread.

**Analytical Critic (Conan Doyle / Christie style)**:
- Check plot logic, fairness of clues, consistency, and misdirection quality.

**Mythic / Literary Reviewer (Tolkien style)**:
- Evaluate thematic depth, beauty of language, and sense of wonder or grandeur.

**Commercial Thriller Reviewer (Lee Child style)**:
- Focus on momentum, competence, and page-turning readability.

**General Quality Reviewer**:
- Check voice consistency, cliché removal, sensory impact, and overall chill factor.
